💳 Beep Card Tips

  • Minimum load: ₱100 for new cards
  • Maximum load: ₱10,000
  • Cards can be used on MRT, LRT, and select buses
  • Tap cards at entrance AND exit - otherwise you'll be charged maximum fare
  • You can reload at any station ticket booth or convenience stores

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do children pay fare?

Children below 3 feet tall (approx. 0.91m) ride for free. Students and senior citizens get 20% discount with valid ID.

What if I exit at a different station?

You'll be charged the fare for your actual exit station. If using Beep Card, always tap out so you're charged correctly.

Can I use one ticket for transfers?

Single journey tickets are for one line only. For transfers, you need a Beep Card or buy separate tickets.
