📍 First-Time Commuter Tips

🚉

Know Your Exit

Large stations like Cubao and EDSA have multiple exits. Check landmarks before going up.

🔄

Transfer Stations

Cubao connects MRT and LRT-2. EDSA/Doroteo Jose connects LRT-1 and LRT-2 via walkway.

🚶

Walking Distance

Some stations look close on map but require long walks. Ask station staff for the nearest exit.

🧭

Direction Matters

Always check if train is heading North or South before boarding.
